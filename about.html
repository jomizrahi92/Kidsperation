About Kidsperation

Kidsperation Inc provides SETSS providers who are special education teachers that can help your child stay in a general education classroom. The SETSS provider may work directly with the child or in a group setting and provide direct specially designed and/or supplemental instruction to the child. The Special Ed teacher may adapt the subject matter ("modified curriculum ") and/or visual aids, highlighted worksheets, simplified directions and other kinds of "modified instruction".
